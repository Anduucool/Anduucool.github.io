Not Yet

The band that never existed

Not Yet was the greatest rock band the world had never heard—born from the restless dreams of two inseparable friends, JP and Anderson. In their minds, they poured every scream, every heartbreak, and every whispered hope into their beautiful songs, filling empty nights with roaring guitars and lyrics that cut like glass. Their songs burned with raw emotion, but they were trapped in a world no one else could touch. Not Yet wasn’t just a band; it was their refuge from something beautiful that would never leave their heads.
